Clean Steps

Locate Dearty

Identify points of dirt, such as submerged garbage cans, garbage bins, clogged channels, and any vast space filled with dirt. Also find out about the names of the city, the quater, the street and of the exact place where this dirt point is located.

Take a Pic

Once you have identified a dirt point according to at least the cases mentioned in step one, take a picture of the dirt point with your smartphone and save the image in an accessible location on your phone.

Fill the Form

After you have collected all the information that could help find this point of dirt, andsaved an image, fill out this form with this informations while being as precise as possible about the location and other details in the description and do not forget to select the images you saved.

Submit

After completing the form, click Submit. This information will be sent to dedicated cleaning companies and a cleaning crew will be sent to do the cleaning. Thank you for contributing to the well-being of our city, you save lives when you fight dirt and we are proud of you!!!
